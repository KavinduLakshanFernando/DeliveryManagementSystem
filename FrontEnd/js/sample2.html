FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DeliveryHub and how it can benefit your business.

How does route optimization work?

Our advanced algorithms calculate the most efficient routes based on various factors including distance, traffic conditions, delivery windows, and driver availability. This helps reduce fuel costs, increase delivery capacity, and improve on-time performance.

Can customers track their deliveries?

Yes, customers receive tracking links via SMS or email that allow them to see the real-time status of their deliveries, including estimated arrival times. This transparency helps improve customer satisfaction and reduces support inquiries.
